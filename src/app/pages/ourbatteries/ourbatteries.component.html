Our Batteries

Platinum Range

A Platinum Battery is a high-performance lead-acid battery designed for premium vehicles and demanding applications. It offers superior longevity, durability, and reliability due to advanced plate technology and enhanced corrosion resistance. These batteries provide higher cold-cranking amps (CCA) for better engine starts in extreme temperatures and are ideal for vehicles with high electrical demands.

Gold Battery

A Gold Battery is a premium-grade lead-acid battery that delivers excellent performance and longevity. It is designed for everyday driving, providing strong starting power and consistent reliability. Gold batteries typically feature improved internal components that enhance charge retention and cycle life, making them suitable for modern vehicles with standard electrical needs.

EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery)

An EFB Battery is an advanced version of a traditional lead-acid battery, specifically engineered for vehicles with start-stop technology. It features thicker plates and an improved electrolyte circulation system, allowing it to handle frequent charge and discharge cycles. EFBs provide faster recharge rates, better durability, and longer lifespan than standard flooded batteries, making them ideal for vehicles with moderate power demands.

AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Battery

An AGM Battery is a high-performance, maintenance-free battery designed for vehicles with advanced electrical systems and start-stop functionality. It uses a special glass mat separator that absorbs the electrolyte, preventing spills and increasing vibration resistance. AGM batteries offer faster charging, deep cycling capabilities, and longer life, making them ideal for luxury vehicles, high-performance cars, and vehicles with heavy electrical loads.
